# SneakerPulse Design C

## Objective
- A dramatic concept with a focus on contrast and movement.

## Target Audience
- Trendsetters who seek standout designs and conversation starters.

## Constraints
- Bold palette with up to two accent colors; typography to be adjusted for legibility on apparel.

## Variant Prompts
- Variant 1: Dynamic motion motif; high-contrast palette.
- Variant 2: Monochrome with kinetic typography.
- Variant 3: Abstract pattern inspired by street art.

## Asset Lists
- Fabric/print specs, sample swatches, brand fonts.

## Data Models
- SKU, print areas, sizing.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Designs demonstrate strong shelf presence; feasible production options; two colorways ready.
