# SneakerPulse Design B

## Objective
- Explore alternative direction with bold type, geometric shapes.

## Target Audience
- Students and young professionals who value street-smart styles.

## Constraints
- Brand tone: bold yet approachable; palette black/gray with accent color; typographic style: bold sans.

## Variant Prompts
- Variant 1: Geometric motif with heavy typography; print-ready geometry.
- Variant 2: Abstract texture with strong color pops.
- Variant 3: Minimalist approach with heavy negative space.

## Asset Lists
- Mockup photos, fabric/color specs, fonts.

## Data Models
- SKU, size runs, material specs.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Clear, production-feasible prompts; colorways match brand palette; at least two assets ready for mockups.
